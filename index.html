About Us

Established in 2008 by a group of passionate engineers, Starlink Engineering Ltd. has grown into a strategic partner for international organizations, telecom operators, corporate enterprises, and government agencies. We are committed to delivering high-quality, on-time, and cost-effective engineering solutions that simplify our clients' operations and add real value.

Starlink is a trusted engineering service provider in Bangladesh, delivering comprehensive, end-to-end solutions across diverse sectors and strategically delivered through following specialized business units:

 Solar EPC,

 Telecom Engineering

 CME & Interior Decoration

 Operation & Maintenance

Our core expertise spans the project lifecycle, encompassing project development, site surveys, feasibility studies, planning, engineering and design, procurement, construction and installation, commissioning and testing, operation and maintenance, optimization and tuning as well as supplying relevant products and accessories.

Our strength lies in our skilled workforce of 150+ professionals, well-trained to manage complex engineering projects with precision, safety, and sustainability. Our integrated approach and diversified capabilities enable us to serve both the public and private sectors in telecommunications, civil infrastructure, and renewable energy.
